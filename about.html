OUR STORY

The Jessica Michelle School of Dance was established in January 2006 and offers a variety of dance classes for children from 2 years upwards. Classes are held at several locations in Rothley, Mountsorrel and Glenfield.

As registered teachers of the British Theatre Dance Association (BTDA),which are members of CDET (Council for Dance Education and Training), our school offers a comprehensive range of dance disciplines and examinations including Ofqual accredited qualifications.
